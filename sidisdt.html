Sidis Dining Table

Walnut live edge dining table. 7/4" thick black walnut top biscuit joined and cut with 15 degree edge profiles. Carved and smoothed live edges on both long sides. Black epoxy used to fill cracks. Finished with Rubio monocoat hardwax applied by hand and mechanically buffed. Steel legs painted black. Steel c channel recessed on bottom side for stability. Built as a set with matching live edge coffee table. Slabs sourced from Thomason Sawmill in Alto, GA and kiln dried at Frost Family Farms in Winston, GA.

W ~39" L 78" H 28".
